Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub Centres

Transforming North West Province with Innovation and Technology

North West Province is embracing the future, positioning itself as a hub for innovation and technology in Southern Africa. With a strategic focus on tech advancement, digital empowerment, and sustainability, the region aims to unlock new opportunities for growth, job creation, and community development.

By investing in cutting-edge technologies, including AI, Agritech, AWS, and more, North West is setting the stage for a digital transformation that will shape the future for generations to come. Innovation is the key to unlocking the potential of industries, fostering economic growth, and creating smarter, more efficient communities.

Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub (MDiHub)

MDiHub is the heart of innovation in North West Province. This is where tech enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, and visionaries come together to push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital world. From hosting workshops and training to facilitating access to digital tools, MDiHub is dedicated to building a more innovative future for the province.

Satellite Offices (Centres)


Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub also has satellite offices (centres) in four districts in the North West province.
In the Ngaka Modiri Molema district, it is where the main centre is located alongside, the Mafikeng Cisco EDGE Centre (Incubation space with a Cisco Networking Academy), Cisco EDGE center is also home to the Microsoft Power Platform in AgriTech SIFA project and Living Lab (grassroots programs & digital literacy).

The Dr Ruth Segomotsi Mompati district lies the AgriTech centre at the Taung Agricultural College which specializes in Strategic technological interventions in agriculture.

The Dr Kenneth Kaunda District, currently based in Klerksdorp is focused on AWS-based solutions with projects spanning Agritech, healthcare, and predictive maintenance for the mining sector.

Bojanala, in Rustenburg is home to the Artificial Intelligence in AgriTech centre. Design, develop, and deploy an AI solution using Microsoft Azure to address a specific agricultural challenge using an innovation.
In the same distric, in Moruleng lies the INSETA centre. The Insurance Sector Education and Training Authority's (INSETA) primary purpose is grow the pool and quality of critical and scarce skills within the insurance sector. These skills enhance the sector while supporting the transformation of the country.
