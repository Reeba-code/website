About Us

Empowering Innovation for Tomorrow

Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub

Mahikeng innovation hub (MIHub) is an innovative co-working techno hub in the heart of Mahikeng, capital city of the North West Province, with it's focus being unearthing and cultivating innovative 4IR solutions that will ultimately assist communities to acclimatize in this vast changing technological world, while improving efficiency. We specialize in;

  • Inclusive, technology-driven development
  • Early start-up Incubation
  • Prototype development
  • Hackathons
  • Skills Development

With the world moving towards seamless and integrated solutions, MIHub has created an innovative platform to support entrepreneurs to drive their business with the latest technology, ignite innovative excellence, build products and solutions that will ultimately bear social, technological and economic benefits to the North West Province while competing on a global stage. “innovate for tomorrow’s world today”

Our Core Values

Innovation

We believe that every problem holds the seed of a powerful solution. Our focus is on turning visionary ideas into impactful digital products.

Community

We are committed to building a tech-savvy community, empowering individuals, and fostering collaboration across diverse backgrounds.

Sustainability

Our initiatives are driven by the goal of creating a resilient tech landscape that is both economically and socially sustainable.

What We Do

We specialize in AI-driven projects, digital skills development, and tech-based startups. Our services include training programs, startup incubation, and collaborative opportunities with industry experts to bring advanced technology solutions to life.

Software Development

We create robust software solutions tailored to solve complex business challenges, using modern programming languages and frameworks to deliver high-quality, scalable products.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Our IoT services connect devices and systems, enabling smarter environments through data-driven insights for real-time decision-making and process optimization.

AgriTech

Leveraging technology for sustainable agriculture, we offer solutions that improve crop yield, streamline farming processes, and enhance resource management.

AWS

Certified AWS expertise to help businesses scale in the cloud, improve their infrastructure, and maximize efficiency with Amazon Web Services.

Microsoft Power Platform

Our team uses Power Platform to build low-code solutions, enabling rapid app development and process automation tailored to meet business needs.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

From machine learning to natural language processing, we implement AI-driven technologies to solve real-world problems and enhance user experiences.

Hackathons

The Mafikeng Digital Innovation Hub Hackathon invites participants to create innovative digital solutions that address community challenges and drive economic growth through technology.

3D Printing

We use 3D printing technology for rapid prototyping, allowing for innovative product designs, faster iteration, and customized manufacturing solutions.

AR & VR

Immersive experiences using augmented and virtual reality, transforming training, entertainment, and educational applications with engaging simulations.

Incubation

Join the most interactive, dynamic, smart platform where technocrats, innovators, businesses, academics and research community collaborate and innovate.
MDIHub offers solutions suited to both Start-ups, locally established businesses, international businesses, Donors and Academic institutions.
